## Black Banana Engagement — After Action Report

**Date:** 2026-04-12
**Participants:** Core team — 4 operators

### The Situation

Surprise CQB engagement in the south compound. Two-hour window, rotating attack teams, tight corridors designed to neutralize any range advantage. Pure trigger discipline and spatial awareness.

### What Happened

Four rotations. We held all four. The second rotation brought a coordinated rush on the east door that caught us moving — Naku read the play early enough to redirect before contact. That call won the rotation.

### Standout Moments

- Gravedigger used a hallway corner to dominate an entire rotation with minimal movement. Smart.
- Team communicated positions faster than the opposition could adjust. Radio discipline = compound defense.

### Areas to Improve

- Rotation 3 left the west window unmonitored for ~30 seconds. One attacker nearly exploited it.
- Reload timing needs to be staggered more consistently during active contact.

### Final Assessment

Dominant performance. The kind of op that builds confidence — and the kind of footage that recruitment posters are made of.
