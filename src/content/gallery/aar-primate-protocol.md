## Op: Primate Protocol — After Action Report

**Date:** 2026-08-15
**Participants:** Full team — 6 operators

### Phase 1: Canopy Advance

Alpha squad pushed through the north treeline on schedule. Two contacts at the first cache — quick, clean, no casualties on our side. Cache secured at H+1:45.

### Phase 2: Extraction Hold

Bravo held the extraction point through three distinct hostile pushes. The second push nearly broke the left flank but Gravedigger's suppression held long enough for Nakunga to plug the gap.

### Lessons Learned

- Comms discipline was the difference in Phase 2. Keep it up.
- The left flank needs a second position marker for dense terrain operations.
- HPA systems ran reliably all day despite the heat.

### Verdict

Death Monkeys: operational. See you at the next one.
