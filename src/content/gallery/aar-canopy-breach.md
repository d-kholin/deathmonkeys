## Op: Canopy Breach — After Action Report (Cancelled)

**Planned Date:** 2026-06-01
**Status:** Cancelled — weather advisory and field closure

### What Happened

Environment Canada issued a severe thunderstorm advisory 48 hours before zero hour. The field called the closure early Friday morning. All participants were notified within the hour.

### Pre-Op Planning Notes

The Canopy Breach plan was well-structured — night assault framework, staged infiltration routes, and solid contingency comms in place. That planning work doesn't disappear when an op gets scrubbed. We carry it forward.

### Lessons from the Cancellation

- Early notification protocol worked well. No one showed up to an empty field.
- Weather monitoring should start 72 hours out for night ops, not 48.
- The ridge infiltration route plan is solid and will be reused when Canopy Breach is rescheduled.
